# Privacy Policy — Snap My Windows

**Last updated:** March 15, 2026

## Overview

Snap My Windows is a free, open-source macOS window management app. Your privacy is important to us — this app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No usage analytics or telemetry is sent
- No crash reports are transmitted
- No network connections are made by the app
- No cookies or tracking technologies are used

## Accessibility Permissions

Snap My Windows requires macOS Accessibility permissions to move and resize windows. This permission is granted by you through System Settings and is used solely to perform window management actions on your Mac. No window content or information is read, recorded, or transmitted.

## Third Parties

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

## Open Source

Snap My Windows is open source. You can review the full source code at [github.com/zachsai/snap-my-windows](https://github.com/zachsai/snap-my-windows).

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.

## Changes

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted in the GitHub repository.
